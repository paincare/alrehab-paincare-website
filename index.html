Understanding Physiotherapy's Approach

Physiotherapy is more than just exercises ‚Äî it's a comprehensive, science-based approach to healing and prevention. At AL-REHAB PAINCARE, we combine three core pillars of treatment to help patients recover faster, move better, and live pain-free.

01

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to improve joint mobility, release tension, and relieve pain.

  • Tennis Elbow: Manual therapy to reduce tendon strain and promote healing
  • Osteoarthritis: Joint mobilization techniques to improve movement and decrease stiffness
  • Rotator Cuff Injuries: Targeted shoulder exercises to restore strength and stability
  • Carpal Tunnel Syndrome: Wrist and hand mobilization combined with ergonomic education
02

Exercise Therapy

Targeted movements to strengthen muscles, enhance flexibility, and restore functional movement.

  • Lower Back Pain: Core strengthening exercises to support the spine and relieve pressure
  • Sciatica: Nerve gliding and stretching techniques to relieve pressure and improve mobility
  • Post-Surgery Rehab: Progressive exercises for safe and effective recovery
  • Sports Injuries: Sport-specific training to return to peak performance
03

Education & Prevention

Guidance on posture, ergonomics, lifestyle changes, and self-care strategies to prevent recurrence.

  • Posture correction and ergonomic advice
  • Home exercise programs for continued progress
  • Lifestyle modifications for long-term wellness
  • Self-care strategies to prevent future injuries
